# Privacy Policy for Santree Go

**Effective Date:** May 20, 2026

At Santree Go, we prioritize your privacy and the security of your data.

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Geolocation Data:** We access your GPS coordinates to display nearby Quranic verses. This data stays within the application's runtime and is not uploaded to our permanent storage unless explicitly required for a game mechanic (e.g., Mosque Hub identification).
* **Account Information:** When you log in via Google or Quran.com, we collect your basic profile information (Name, Email, UID) to maintain your XP, Rank, and Collections.

## 2. How We Use Data
* To personalize your Quranic discovery experience.
* To maintain and display your streaks and progress.
* To sync your bookmarks back to your Quran.com account.

## 3. Data Storage
We use **Firebase** for localized profile storage and the **Quran Foundation API** for syncing religious progress. We do not sell or trade your personal data.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We integrate with:
* **Quran Foundation APIs:** For Quranic content and User features.
* **Open-Meteo:** To provide weather-contextual verses (Location data is sent anonymously).
* **Mapbox/Leaflet:** For displaying the map interface.

## 5. Your Rights
You can request the deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us at **santreedigitalid@gmail.com**.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **santreedigitalid@gmail.com**.

## 7. Policy Changes
We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our app features or legal requirements.
